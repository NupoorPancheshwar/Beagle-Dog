The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a bread of small scent hound,similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare,known aas beagling. Processing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts,the beagle is the primary breed used as a detecton dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper,and a lack of inherited health problems.

Trivia

  1. Height - 13-16 in (33-41 cm)
  2. Weight - 22-25 lb (10.0-11.3kg )
  3. Coat -Short haired, hard coat of medium length
  4. Colour -Tricolor or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan
  5. Life span - 12-15 years
